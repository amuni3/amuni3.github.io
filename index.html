Aneri Muni


I'm an Undergraduate Research Assistant at Georgia Institute of Technology majoring in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Robotics. I recently spent three months as a visiting student researcher at the Autonomous Systems Lab in ETH Zurich, through the ThinkSwiss Research Scholarship. While there, I worked on developing a block stacking routine for ABB's YuMi robot.

Previously, I completed a Robotics and Image Processing co-op with the Food Processing Technology Division under Georgia Tech Research Institute, where I developed autonomy algorithms for a robotic system to monitor chickens in a poultry grow out house.

For my Undergraduate thesis, I am designing a software to create 3D models of chickens to calculate the correlation between size and weight of birds.

When I'm not working with robots and computers, I love reading, hiking and painting!
